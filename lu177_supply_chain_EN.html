2023 shortage — the bottleneck was not the isotope
The 2023 ¹⁷⁷Lu-PSMA shortage was not caused by a lack of bulk isotope, but by a saturation of radiolabeling capacity. Since marketing authorizations are site-specific, no automatic transfer between sites is possible. The weak link is regulatory, not reactor-based.
